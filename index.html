Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and safety, with a particular focus on large language models (LLMs). I am deeply interested in exploring the adversarial robustness of LLMs, specifically examining vulnerabilities such as jailbreaks, prompt injections, data poisoning, and backdoors. I am currently focused on developing techniques for improving the security, reliability, and ethical use of AI. This involves research into methods for detecting, mitigating, and preventing malicious interactions with LLMs, as well as advancing transparency and controllability in AI.
